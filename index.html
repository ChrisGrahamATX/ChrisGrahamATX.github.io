About Me


As a UX/UI Designer, my expertise lies in creating user-friendly designs that prioritize accessibility.

With personal experience of being diagnosed with ADHD at a young age, I consistently highlight the significance of inclusivity and neurodiversity in my UX decisions, following a "less is more" approach to provide the most satisfying user experience via the simplest approaches.


Outside the office, I've been exploring design through creative projects, and I'm excited to soon take a furniture design class. Beyond my designer role, I'm a gamer, guitar player, fine artist, foodie, hiker, and traveler. I look forward to sharing more stories with you.
